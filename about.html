Our Story

From a small team to a global platform

Nexlance was founded in 2019 by three developers frustrated by how hard it was for non-technical founders to build a professional online presence. We believed anyone with a great idea deserved a great website ‚Äî without hiring a developer or learning to code.

Starting from a tiny office in Bangalore, we bootstrapped our way to our first 10,000 users. Two years later, we raised our Series A and expanded globally. Today, Nexlance powers over 12 million websites across 180 countries and is trusted by freelancers, agencies, and Fortune 500 companies alike.

We're still on that same mission: make powerful web tools radically simple. And we're just getting started.
