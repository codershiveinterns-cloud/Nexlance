Nexlance wins "Best Website Builder" at India SaaS Awards 2025

March 11, 2025

Nexlance has been recognized as Best Website Builder at the India SaaS Awards 2025, highlighting the platform's product innovation, user experience, and customer satisfaction.

The award reflects momentum built through strong adoption among creators, entrepreneurs, and small businesses across the region.

Nexlance says the recognition reinforces its mission to make web creation faster, simpler, and more accessible to every type of user.
