Nexlance surpasses 4 million active users, announces Southeast Asia expansion

September 3, 2025

Nexlance has crossed 4 million active users and announced its next growth phase across Southeast Asia. The company is opening offices in Singapore and Kuala Lumpur to support regional customers and local partnerships.

The expansion reflects growing demand from creators and SMBs looking for flexible website tools, localized support, and faster digital onboarding.

Nexlance plans to continue investing in regional product enhancements, language support, and customer success programs throughout the Asia-Pacific market.
