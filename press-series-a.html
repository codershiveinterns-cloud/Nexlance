Nexlance closes $14M Series A, expands team to 150 employees

October 5, 2024

Nexlance announced a $14 million Series A round led by Sequoia India to accelerate engineering growth, market expansion, and platform maturity as it crossed 1 million users.

The investment supports team expansion across product, support, and go-to-market functions as the company builds for the next stage of scale.

Nexlance says the funding will help sharpen its focus on creator tools, e-commerce workflows, and AI-driven website experiences.
