Nexlance raises $28M Series B to accelerate AI-powered web creation

January 15, 2026

Nexlance today announced a $28 million Series B funding round led by Tiger Global with participation from Sequoia India and Accel Partners. The new investment will help the company expand AI-assisted website creation, strengthen product infrastructure, and accelerate international growth.

With this round, Nexlance's total funding reaches $42 million. The company plans to deepen its investment in template intelligence, faster publishing workflows, and business tools that help creators and small businesses go live with less friction.

The funding will also support new hiring across product, engineering, and customer success teams as Nexlance scales its platform for a growing global customer base.
