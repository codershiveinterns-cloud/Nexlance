Nexlance launches AI Design Assistant — build a complete website in under 5 minutes

November 8, 2025

Nexlance has launched its AI Design Assistant, a feature that turns plain-language prompts into complete website drafts. The new system helps users start faster by generating layouts, sections, and starter content from a short brief.

The assistant is now available across plans and is designed to help individuals, freelancers, and growing businesses create polished websites with less manual setup.

With this release, Nexlance is expanding its AI-first workflow to support faster onboarding, better personalization, and more confident design decisions for non-technical users.
