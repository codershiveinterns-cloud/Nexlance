Nexlance Commerce 2.0: zero transaction fees and built-in POS system

June 22, 2025

Nexlance has introduced Commerce 2.0, a major platform update that removes transaction fees and adds built-in point-of-sale support for merchants selling online and offline.

The release is aimed at helping businesses simplify payments, inventory handling, and customer checkout across channels.

Commerce 2.0 expands Nexlance's focus on practical business tools for merchants who want a single platform to manage storefronts, orders, and growth.
