Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

1. OVERVIEW

This Refund Policy ("Policy") explainsNexlance's refund and return practices for subscriptions and purchases made throughNexlance.com. This Policy should be read together with our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

For purchases processed through Paddle as Merchant of Record, Paddle's refund policies also apply. Please review https://www.paddle.com/legal for details.

2. REFUND ELIGIBILITY

2.1 Refundable Purchases

  • Monthly subscription fees
  • Annual subscription fees
  • One-time license purchases
  • Upgrade fees
  • Add-on services

2.2 Non-Refundable Items

  • Custom development services (once completed)
  • Training and consulting services (once delivered)
  • Professional services and support hours used
  • Data migration services (once completed)
  • API access fees (once activated)
  • Taxes, VAT, and remitted transaction fees

3. REFUND TIMELINE

3.1 Standard Refund Period

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee:

  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase.
  • Applies only to the first month of service.
  • Requests after 30 days are generally not eligible.

3.2 Exceptions

  • Service failure exceeding 7 consecutive days
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges
  • Service discontinuation (pro-rata refund)
  • Regulatory requirements

3.3 Annual Subscriptions

  • Eligible within 30 days of purchase
  • Full refund or full credit depending on payment method
  • Partial refund possible within first 90 days (remaining months)

4. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

Step 1: Contact Support

Email: admin@nexlance.com

Subject Line: "Refund Request"

Step 2: Provide Details

  • Order number and transaction ID
  • Original purchase date
  • Clear explanation of issue
  • Attach screenshots if applicable

Step 3: Review Process

Requests are reviewed within 5-7 business days.

Step 4: Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 10-15 business days. Paddle transactions may take an additional 3-5 business days.

5. REFUND CONDITIONS & RESTRICTIONS

Eligibility Conditions

  • Valid active account
  • Request submitted within timeframe
  • No violation of Terms of Service
  • No misuse or competing product usage

Restrictions

  • Policy violations
  • Fraudulent activity or chargebacks
  • Refund abuse history
  • Request by unauthorized person

Data Deletion

Upon refund approval, your account will be terminated and data deleted within 30 days. Back up important data before requesting a refund.

6. SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION

You may cancel at any time. Cancellation applies to future billing periods only. No refunds for current billing period.

7. PADDLE MERCHANT OF RECORD

Paddle handles payment processing, tax collection, invoicing, and refund execution for transactions processed via Paddle.

8. CHARGEBACKS & DISPUTES

Initiating a chargeback without contacting us first may result in account suspension or termination.

9. SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

In case of service discontinuation, pro-rata refunds will be issued.

10. CREDIT & SERVICE CREDITS

We may offer account credits, service extensions, or plan upgrades instead of cash refunds in certain cases.

11. TAXES & FEES

Taxes and transaction fees already remitted to authorities are non-refundable.

12. INTERNATIONAL CUSTOMERS

Customers in the EU/EEA and UK may have additional statutory refund rights.

13. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I get a refund after 30 days?

Generally no, unless exceptional circumstances apply.

How long does a refund take?

10–15 business days after approval.

14. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of updates.
