Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy explains how Nexlance ("Nexlance", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our website and CRM services available at Nexlance.com.

By accessing or using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, phone number, and company information
  • Account credentials and user preferences
  • Billing information (processed securely through Paddle)
  • Support communications and feedback
  • Customer or business data uploaded into the CRM platform

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you access our website or platform, certain information may be collected automatically through cookies, analytics tools, and server logs.

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and usage data
  • Approximate location (country or city level)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies
  • Application performance and diagnostic logs

2.3 Third-Party Sources

We may receive information about you from trusted third-party services that help us operate our business.

  • Paddle (payment processing and billing)
  • Analytics and hosting providers
  • Your organization or employer (if applicable)
  • Publicly available business directories

3. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a contract
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Consent (where required by law)

4. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Provide and operate CRM services
  • Manage subscriptions, billing, and payments
  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve platform performance and analytics
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • Send marketing communications (with consent)

5. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell personal data. However, we may share information with trusted service providers when necessary to operate our services.

  • Paddle (Merchant of Record for payment processing)
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Analytics and monitoring services
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Professional advisers such as auditors or lawyers

6. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your information may be transferred and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or similar legal mechanisms to protect personal data.

7. DATA RETENTION

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

  • Account data retained during active subscription
  • Billing records retained for approximately 6–7 years
  • Support communications retained for 12–24 months
  • CRM data deleted after account termination unless legally required

8. COOKIES AND TRACKING

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our website, remember user preferences, analyze traffic, and improve user experience. You may control cookie settings through your browser.

9. SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect personal data, including:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Access controls and authentication safeguards
  • Security monitoring and intrusion detection
  • System logging and security auditing

10. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA), you may have the right to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Request data portability

11. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our website may contain links to third-party services. Those services operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

13. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
